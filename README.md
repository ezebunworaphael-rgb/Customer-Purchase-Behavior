## Key Insights
Male customers contribute 44% the total revenue due to larger customer base.
Sales peak during  spring at $160k approximately indicating strong seasonality.
Clothing and Accessories are the top performing categories suggesting customer preference. these categories should be prioritized for inventory and promotions
 New York leads in revenue, while some regions underperform.
